Subdivision Conveyancing Services

First Class Legal’s subdivision services cover every aspect of the development process, ensuring compliance and efficiency. Below is a detailed overview of their offerings.

Section 32 and Contract Drafting

They prepare accurate Section 32 statements and contracts with special conditions for subdivisions, such as staged settlements or shared infrastructure clauses. This prevents sale voids, critical in high-value projects.

Lender Coordination

They liaise with lenders to align financing or mortgage discharges with subdivision timelines, ensuring funds are ready for settlement.

Electronic Plan Lodgment

They lodge plans of subdivision with land registries like Land Victoria or Titles Queensland, ensuring compliance with council requirements, such as minimum lot sizes or access easements.

Owners Corporation Creation

For multi-unit developments, they establish owners corporations, handling legal setups for shared facilities like driveways or utilities, ensuring compliance with the Owners Corporations Act 2006 (VIC).
